Privacy Policy

App: Glided Gate Puzzle  |  Developer: Yash Dev  |  Effective date: March 15, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Glided Gate Puzzle ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application. By playing Glided Gate Puzzle, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information through two third-party services integrated into the app:

a) Firebase Analytics (Google)

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. Firebase may automatically collect:

  • Device model, operating system version, and language
  • App version and session duration
  • In-app events (e.g. level start, level complete, hint used, Time Attack streak)
  • Approximate country/region (derived from IP address)
  • A randomly generated Firebase installation ID

This data is used solely to improve gameplay and user experience. Firebase Analytics does not collect your name, email, or precise location. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

b) Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display ads in the app. AdMob may collect and use:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID / GAID) to serve personalized ads
  • IP address (used to infer approximate location for ad targeting)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, view time)
  • App usage patterns relevant to ad serving

You may opt out of personalized ads by visiting your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization. For more information, see Google's Ads Policy.

3. Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device using Android SharedPreferences and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game progress (current level, saved game state)
  • Best scores, move counts, and star ratings
  • Daily play streak
  • Sound and vibration preferences
  • Time Attack best streak

This data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Permissions Used

  • Vibrate — Haptic feedback during gameplay (toggleable in settings)
  • Post Notifications — Optional daily play reminders (you can deny or disable at any time)
  • Receive Boot Completed — Reschedules daily reminders after device restart
  • Internet — Required for loading ads and sending analytics events
  • Access Network State — Checks connectivity before loading ads

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with:

  • Google Firebase — for analytics processing
  • Google AdMob — for serving advertisements

Both services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Glided Gate Puzzle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention

Firebase Analytics retains event data for up to 14 months by default. AdMob data retention is governed by Google's policies. Local game data is retained until you uninstall the app.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (see Section 2b)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
